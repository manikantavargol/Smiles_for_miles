Mullayanagiri Peak – The City of Diverse Existence

Mullayanagiri Peak is located in the Chandra Drona range and attracts numerous adventure seekers every year. The altitude of the peak is about 2000 meters. Mullayanagiri Peak has amazing natural trekking trails, surrounded by dense flora and is renowned as the highest peak in Karnataka in Chikmagalur. There are several points at this peak that offer breathtaking views while trekking. Visitors can also enjoy hiking and camping along with the trekking here.

Hebbe Waterfalls– In Harmony with Nature

Hebbe falls is a unique place to visit in chikmagalur for couples and with friends to enjoy a perfect day. It is a perfect place to relax and enjoy natural scenery. Enjoy trekking at the natural trails that lead to the wonderful waterfalls. It is believed that its water contains medicinal herbs that cure various diseases. Biologists and scientists across the globe visit this place to research on the medicinal property of the water. The height of the fall is about 170 m high.

Baba Budan Giri– Queer but Captivating

Baba Budan Giri is a renowned peak and a tourist destination that was named after the Muslim saint Baba Budan. The peak is also known as Chandra Drona Parvatha and has 3 caves that are considered pious. These peaks are covered with rich greenery and have various varieties of flowers. Baba Budan Giri is known for its amazing forest range and is also famous for hiking and trekking along the natural trails. It is one of the best spots to visit in Chikmagalur for the adventure freaks.

Hirekolale Lake– An Architectural Marvel

Hirekolale Lake is a man-made reservoir located in the Chikmagalur district of Karnataka and one of the best scenic spots in India. Overlooking the panorama of mountains in the Western Ghats, Hirekolale Lake is one of the must-visit tourist attractions in Chikmagalur. The tranquil environment, pleasant weather, and dense nature all around are simply jaw-dropping for every spectator in Chikmagalur. This lake was constructed to supply drinking water to Chikmagalur and also for agriculture and irrigation of lands in the neighboring villages. Hirekolale Lake offers stunning views of the landscapes especially during the sunset, the rays scattering in the atmosphere are a beauty to behold. It also becomes a delight for photography lovers who look to add some best sunset shots to their camera. The best part is that one can also enjoy the excellent views of Mullayanagiri Hills from this Lake. Hirekolale Lake can be visited throughout the year and there is no such entry fee to visit the lake. While visiting this beautiful spot carry a sufficient amount of edible items as there are no such eateries around the lake.

Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary

Get away from the hustle-bustle of city life and enjoy a peaceful trip to the Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary, a heaven for nature lovers. This Wildlife Sanctuary is spread over an area of 490 square kilometers and has the highest tiger population along with rich flora. The Sanctuary is also home to various endangered birds and wild animals such as deer, leopard, etc. Climb the Kallathigiri Mountain to enjoy the mesmerizing view from the top of the hill.

Jhari Waterfalls – Nature’s Fury and Peace

Jhari Waterfall, also known as Buttermilk Falls is a beautiful place to visit with your loved ones. Surrounded by dense forest and coffee plantations it is one of the most visited waterfalls located near Attigundi in Chikmagalur district of Karnataka. It falls en route to Babam Budangiri or Kemmungandi. The most interesting fact of this waterfall is that it is formed by springs originating in the mountains. Getaway from the busy city life and unwind in the lap of nature. A pool formed by a waterfall below allows the tourist to take a dip into the cool water, swim and play.

Coffee Museum – Tranquility at its Best

Explore the Coffee Museum and know the history of coffee plantations in South India. The museum runs under the Coffee Board of India and exhibits various documents and maps that provide information about the processes and other coffee growing regions in India. Get complete knowledge about the coffee-making process starting from plucking and ending to powdering of the coffee beans.

Sringeri Sharada Peetham – Relax Away From All The Chaos

Sringeri Sharada Peetham is a renowned monastery established by Adi Shankara in the 8th century. Located on the banks of the Tunga river, it is one of the best Chikmagalur tourist places where travelers can revitalize their weary souls amidst the peace and tranquil surroundings of the temple.

Manikyadhara Falls – Explore This Hidden Hamlet

One of the best parts about Chikmaglur is that there are plenty of waterfalls in the vicinity. One of the natural beauties is Manikyadhara Falls is located next to Baba Budan Giri Hills. The region boasts of impressive trekking trails and stupendous landscapes where you can enjoy a small day picnic with your family. Even during the summer, the temperature of this waterfall remains refreshingly cold. So, make sure that you explore this fall on your vacation as it is counted among the top places to see in Chikmagalur.
